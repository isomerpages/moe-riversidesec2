<p>The <strong>Riverside Alumni</strong> serves as a vibrant platform to engage
ex-Riversidians. We aim to forge lasting links with our graduates who share
a deep passion for service and a commitment to contributing to school excellence.</p>
<p><strong>Our Vision &amp; Mission</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Vision:</strong> A strong network of engaged Riverside Alumni,
collectively contributing to the excellence of our school community.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Mission:</strong> To foster meaningful partnerships between Alumni
and the school, nurturing compassionate, future-ready global citizens.</p>
</li>
</ul>
