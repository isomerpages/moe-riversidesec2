<col>
<col>
<col>
<col>

<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>DSA Main Category</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>DSA Talent Area</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Selection Criteria</strong>
</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p><strong>Selection Process</strong>
</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Leadership and Uniformed Groups</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Leadership and Character</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Applicant must hold leadership roles, for e.g., prefects and CCA leaders,
in his/her primary school, and has led school projects.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The school will refer to:
<br>• School reports - Academic ability and conduct indicated in school reports
and other specific teacher reports.</p>
<p>• Any other student leadership-related records of participation and level
of representation.</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Interview: in-person
<br>- Assessment of attitude and aptitude</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Language and Humanities</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Tamil Language</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Applicant must be able to demonstrate strong interest and talent beyond
the abilities of students in the same age group and have a history of consistent
high academic achievements in <strong>Higher</strong> Tamil Language.</p>
<p>The school will refer to:
<br>• School reports - Academic ability and conduct indicated in school reports
and other specific teacher reports.</p>
<p>• Accomplishment - Evidence in the form of awards or experiences in relevant
contexts (e.g. competitions at national level) or any equivalent demonstration
of talent.</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Interview: in-person
<br>- On-site written assessment
<br>- Assessment of attitude and aptitude</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="2" colspan="1">
<p>Performing Arts</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="2" colspan="1">
<p>• Choir</p>
<p>• Concert Band</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="2" colspan="1">
<p>• Applicant must be able to demonstrate strong interest and talent in
music beyond the abilities of students in the same age group.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The school will refer to:
<br>• School reports - Academic ability and conduct indicated in school reports
and other specific teacher reports.</p>
<p>• &nbsp; Qualification(s) in music, e.g., relevant certificates (if any).</p>
<p>• Accomplishment - Evidence in the form of awards, performances, competitions
(e.g. SYF) or experiences in relevant contexts.</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="2" colspan="1">
<p>• Audition and interview: in-person
<br>- On-site performance assessment
<br>- Demonstration of aptitude and potential
<br>- Assessment of attitude and suitability</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr></tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Visual Arts, Design and Media</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Media</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Applicant must be able to demonstrate strong interest and talent in
the areas of photography, videography, and media production beyond the
abilities of students in the same age group.</p>
<p>&nbsp;The school will refer to:
<br>• School reports - Academic ability and conduct indicated in school reports
and other specific teacher reports.</p>
<p>• Qualification(s) in photography, videography and media production, e.g.,
relevant certificates (if any).</p>
<p>• Accomplishment - Evidence in the form of awards or experiences in relevant
contexts (e.g. competitions, school/public showcases).</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Audition and interview: in-person
<br>- On-site assessment
<br>- Demonstration of aptitude and potential
<br>- Assessment of attitude and suitability</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>STEM</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Applicant must be able to demonstrate strong interest and talent in
an area of STEM (e.g. robotics/programming, Math and Science), beyond the
abilities of students in the same age group.</p>
<p>The school will refer to:</p>
<p>• School reports - Academic ability and conduct indicated in school reports
and other specific teacher reports.</p>
<p>• Qualification(s) in robotics/programming (Lego EV3, Mindstorms, Spike
Prime), e.g., relevant certificates (if any).</p>
<p>• Accomplishment - Evidence in the form of awards or experiences in relevant
contexts such as STEM competitions or projects in school/external events
(if any).</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>• Audition and interview: in-person
<br>- On-site assessment
<br>- Demonstration of aptitude and potential
<br>- Assessment of attitude and suitability</p>
</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="4">
<p><strong><em>Please note that meeting all the criteria does not guarantee the student will be shortlisted/given an offer.</em></strong>
</p>
<p><strong><em>Applicants who do not have prior experience may also apply. The school will assess applicants based on the selection criteria, which may include assessing the potential of the applicants.</em></strong>
</p>
<p><strong><em>You will receive notification from the school by 18 Jul 2025, if your child has been shortlisted to undergo Direct School Admission (DSA) selection. All applicants will receive their application outcomes between 25 Aug and 4 Sep 2025.</em></strong>
</p>
</td>
</tr>
