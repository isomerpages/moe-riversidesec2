<p><u>Online Purchase</u>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Parents are strongly encouraged to make their purchases online via <a href="https://www.pacificbookstores.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.pacificbookstores.com/</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Payment options:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>VISA, MASTERCARD credit/ debit cards, or PayNow, NTUC BTS Fund (e-voucher).</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents can opt for home delivery or self-collection.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>For home delivery, a delivery charge of $14.90 applies.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For self-collection, the dates are available on the website.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The last date for online purchase is 24<sup>th</sup>&nbsp;December 2025.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents are advised to place orders early. Home delivery and self-collection
slots are available on first-come-first-serve basis.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><u>On-site Purchase</u>

</ul>
</li>
</ul>
